# Customer Discovery Summary

## Project Title

Movie Recommendation System

## Target Users Studied

* **University Students:** Frequently watch movies and use streaming platforms.
* **Movie Enthusiasts:** Regularly explore and search for new movies.
* **Streaming Platform Users:** Experience challenges finding suitable content from large movie libraries.

## Number of Responses / Interviews

* **University Students:** 12 Actual (12 Planned)
* **Movie Enthusiasts:** 5 Actual (5 Planned)
* **Streaming Platform Users:** 5 Actual (5 Planned)
* **Total Interviews:** 22 Completed

## Main Repeated Problem

Users struggle to find movies that match their interests because streaming platforms provide too many choices. Many respondents reported spending a long time browsing movies before making a decision and often feeling overwhelmed by the available options.

## Main Current Workarounds

* **Students:** Asking friends for recommendations, searching Google, and browsing streaming platforms manually.
* **Movie Enthusiasts:** Reading reviews, following movie influencers, and checking movie-rating websites.
* **Streaming Platform Users:** Using trending lists and platform-generated recommendations.

## Strongest Evidence Found

* **High-Frequency Pain Points:** "Too many movie choices" received **11 mentions** and "Time spent searching for movies" received **10 mentions**, both categorized as High severity.
* **User Frustration:** Many respondents reported spending more time searching for movies than actually watching them.

## Weak Evidence or Unclear Findings

* **Recommendation Accuracy:** Some users were satisfied with existing recommendation features, while others found them ineffective.
* **Watchlist Usage:** Not all respondents regularly use watchlists, making this feature less critical for the MVP.

## What We Must Revise

The problem has been validated, but the MVP should focus on providing simple and personalized movie recommendations. Instead of including many advanced features, the system should prioritize genre preferences, user interests, and easy movie discovery.

## Updated Problem Statement

Movie viewers often struggle to find movies that match their interests because of the large number of choices available on streaming platforms. Customer discovery interviews revealed repeated complaints about excessive browsing time and difficulty discovering relevant content. As a result, users experience frustration, decision fatigue, and a less enjoyable movie-watching experience. A personalized movie recommendation system could help users discover suitable movies more quickly and efficiently.
