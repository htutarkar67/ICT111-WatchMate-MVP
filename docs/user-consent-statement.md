# User Consent Statement

This prototype has been developed for educational purposes as part of the **ICT111 Fundamental Technology Entrepreneurship** course project for **WatchMate - AI Movie Recommendation System**. You are invited to participate in a usability test by interacting with the AI chatbot, searching for movies, viewing recommendations, and providing feedback on your experience.

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop the test at any time without any penalty. Please do **not** provide any sensitive personal information (such as passwords, national ID numbers, payment information, or financial details). All information collected will be used solely for improving the prototype and completing the course project.

---

## Information Collected

The following information may be collected during the usability test:

- **Name, nickname, or anonymous ID** (optional)
- **Movie preferences** (such as favorite genres or actors)
- **Usability feedback score**
- **Comments on the AI chatbot and recommendation quality**
- **Task completion results and interaction with the recommendation system**

---

## Use of Data

The collected information will be used only for:

- Analyzing usability test results and user satisfaction.
- Improving the AI chatbot, movie recommendation quality, and search functionality.
- Supporting the final ICT111 project report and presentation.

All responses will be treated confidentially and used for academic purposes only.
